Founder & Director

Ashutosh Yadav founded Ashutosh Career Point with a vision to provide modern, affordable and quality education to students. The main aim of opening this coaching institute is to help students achieve success in competitive exams, academics and technical skills.

He believes that every student has talent and with proper mentorship, guidance and motivation they can achieve great success in life. ACP focuses not only on studies but also on confidence, discipline and career growth.
